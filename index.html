Hydroclimate–Society Lab HCSO Lab

The Hydroclimate–Society Lab at the University of Manitoba connects hydroclimate science, data analytics, and societal applications. We develop data-driven methods to understand climate risks, forecast extremes, and link climate information with water, food, and environmental systems to support resilient and sustainable futures.
