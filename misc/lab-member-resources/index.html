Fellowships and Scholarships

The (deadline) is based on the last competition and may differ from the current one.

  • (May 2) University of Manitoba Graduate Fellowship (UMGF) provides $20,000 per year for 12–24 months for master’s and $25,000 per year for 12, 24, 36, or 48 months for doctoral students. The Civil Engineering deadline for application is May 2, 2025.

  • (Oct 17) NSERC Canada Graduate Research Scholarship - Doctoral (CGRS-D) provides $40,000 per year for 36 months. Deadline is October 17 (results announced April 30).

    • Eligibility: Up to 15% of awards are available to international applicants.

  • (Nov 10) Research Manitoba - Master’s Studentship Award provides $20,000 for one year. The most recent application deadline was November 10, 2025, with assessor letters and transcripts due November 24, 2025. Decision results will be announced in May 2026.

    • Eligibility: There are no restrictions for international students.

  • (Nov 10) Research Manitoba - PhD Research Studentship Award provides $25,000 for one year. The most recent application deadline was November 10, 2025, with assessor letters and transcripts due November 24, 2025. Decision results will be announced in May 2026.

    • Eligibility: There are no restrictions for international students.

  • (Dec 1) NSERC Canada Graduate Research Scholarship - Masters (CGRS-M) provides $27,000 for 12 months. Deadline is December 1.

    • Eligibility: Canadian citizen, a permanent resident of Canada or a Protected Person

  • Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships provides $50,000 per year for three years during doctoral studies.

Tuition

  • International Graduate Student Entrance Scholarship (IGSES): Automatically awarded to newly admitted international graduate students with excellent admission GPAs (based on the GPA used for admission). No separate application is required. Typically valued at $5,000–$15,000 and applied in the first year of study.

  • UM’s International Graduate Student Scholarship (IGS): Automatically assessed each term for continuing full-time international graduate students who maintain strong academic performance (usually based on the previous academic year’s GPA). The value can be up to the amount of the international differential tuition fee, effectively reducing tuition to the domestic.

Undergraduate Research Assistant

  • (Mid Feb) University of Manitoba Undergraduate Research Awards (URA) provides $7,000 and is recognized as a UM Co-Curricular Record activity. 16 weeks from May - August. You better consult with me before even the application period opens.
